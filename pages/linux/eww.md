# eww

> Implement your own custom widgets.
> More information: <https://elkowar.github.io/eww>.

- Start the daemon:

`eww daemon`

- Open a widget:

`eww open {{/path/to/directory}}`

- Close a widget:

`eww close {{/path/to/directory}}`

- Reload the configuration:

`eww reload`

- Kill the daemon:

`eww kill`

- Print and watch logs:

`eww logs`
